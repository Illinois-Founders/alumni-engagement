Charitable Contributions

Founders is the premier student-run entrepreneurship nonprofit of UIUC. We are now proud to accept tax-deductable donations that support the programs we have been growing since 2013. We could not have reached this point without the dedication of you, our alumni, throughout the years. If you are able, please consider contributing to the success of our fall events and microgrant program through our donation platform.

Founders Fall Programming

All Founders fall events will be 100% virtual this year due to COVID-19. Below are the dates of each event and a traditional description of what the event stands for.

Startup Bootcamp

Sept 14 - Nov 6

"Startup Bootcamp is an 8-week workshop series designed to lay the groundwork for student entrepreneurial succeess. Lectures will be delivered by leading university faculty and successful members of the entrepreneurship community."

54 Startup Weekend

Oct 9 - 11

"54 is a 3-day event where students from different backgrounds come together to form teams and launch a successful startup. No experience required. Just bring yourself, some friends and good ideas."

Forge | Chicago

Nov 7 - 8

"Forge is a 2-day conference to bring together the entrepreneurship communities across the Midwest. Over a weekend in Chicago, you'll meet other student entrepreneurs from all of the top schools in the region."
