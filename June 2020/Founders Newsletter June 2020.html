Campus Ventures
“Campus Ventures connects university admissions teams to high-achieving highschoolers they want but struggle to recruit—especially first-generation and underrepresented minority groups.”
Founder:

Monica Lannom

Telia Farell-Rosen

Jenna Mukametkhan

Charmaine Nieves

Campus Ventures is bridging the gap between underrepresented students from minority and low economic backgrounds to help prepare them for success at selective universities. They aim to be a central hub for college application resources and future career paths to promote social mobility for future generations.

Using the Technology Entrepreneur Center as a catalyst, founder Monica Lannom remembers the impact the 2020 Silicon Valley Workshop had on her entrepreneurial journey. “It was inspiring to meet with founders that were once in your shoes,” she recollected.

Campus Ventures plans to solidify their business model through deep customer discovery this summer with iVenture. By the end of the accelerator they hope to have a pilot initiative to assert the success of their program.
